Expository

"Some Results Regarding Finite Linear Cellular Automata Modulo Prime Powers" My thesis, written to fulfill my bachelor's degree in science with honours. "An Introduction to Nontransitive Games" Introduces various nontransitive games/phenomena and the math behind them. Written for a Probability course. "A Friendly Introduction to Ray Marching" Introduces and motivates the ray marching algorithm for rendering shapes, as well as provides a sample implementation. Written for a Numerical Methods course. "A Brief Walk Through the Fractal Landscape" Introduces several types of fractals and ways to handle them semi-rigorously. Written for a Fractals and Metric Spaces course.

"The Connection Between Setting and Power in The English Patient" Explores power and setting in The English Patient. This essay won Acadia's "Class Of 1907 Essay Prize" as decided by the Department of English and Theatre for being the best regularly required class essay.
