About Me

Hey! I'm Zach Strong, a current mathematics and computer science undergraduate at Acadia University in Wolfville, NS, Canada. I currently work as a research assistant for Acadia's Department of Mathematics and Statistics, though I also do freelance programming and video editing work.

I code things of all kinds! Some of my larger projects include the Scares Scrambler, software that scrambles the code/data of computer files to create experimental artwork, and LINCELLAUT, a collection of CLI tools for studying the dynamics of finite linear cellular automata. You can read more about these projects on the projects page (currently unfinished).

On top of software development, I also spend a great deal of time creating various types of content online, including comedy/review content on YouTube, experimental music on Bandcamp, and livestreams on Twitch. I've also included an assortment of my other artistic endeavours throughout this site (currently unfinished).
